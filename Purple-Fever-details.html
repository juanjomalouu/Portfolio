Purple Fever

Purple Fever is an speedrun-focuse game where you have to kill all enemies to beat the level. Try to learn all levels to beat your own time and survive all traps and enemies around those cartoon-friendly style maps!

In this project, I took on the responsibility of developing all its features myself, including the interface, gameplay, 3D models and more.
