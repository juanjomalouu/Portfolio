Rolling Beats

Rolling Beats is a rhythm-based videogame where you control Tina Rulles, a skater whose dream is to become the first person to skate in Saturn's rings! Skate accross the song to achieve the maximum score and stay at the top of the leaderboard!

In this project, I was choose by my partners to be the project's director and investigate a way to create a beat detector to create all levels, also, I have to build all levels and make them dynamic and consistent.
